TERRA

TERRA is a newly established company. They sell beauty products which are built from natural materials. Terra means water which relates with nature. The challenge was to create a strong, clean wordmark logo which conveys sense of softness, femininity. The final logo concept from word "TERRA" fits well with the brand and clearly communicates what the brand is all about. Some negative space is used to fit a star in letter "A" expressing nature and beauty at the same time.
